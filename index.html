About

Hi, I'm Max. I'm a self-taught front-end developer. In the summer of 2017 I really started to learn to code. After learning HTML I quickly saw the powers of CSS. The animations just blew my mind. Thankfully I started to learn when css grid and flexbox came out, which makes styling a lot of fun.

After learning the ins and out of ES6, I now spend my time between FreeCodeCamp, solidifying my knowledge with Eloquent Javascript and making websites for friends, family and myself. When I'm not coding you find me drinking fine beers, hiking or eating weird food.
